We apply computational and evolutionary approaches, involving the analysis of high-throughput genomic and transcriptomic data, to fundamental questions in biomedical research on human diseases, namely cancer and ageing-related pathologies. We also build open tools for assisting non-computational scientists in their analyses of transcriptomic data.
